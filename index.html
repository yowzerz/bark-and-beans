About Us...

Why this business?

Bark & Beans is a unique and compassionate pet cafe that combines the love for animals with the joy of delicious food and beverages. Our mission is to create a welcoming space for pet lovers while making a positive impact on the lives of animals in need. With every purchase made at Bark & Beans, 50% of the proceeds go directly to local animal shelters, providing crucial support for the well-being and care of animals who are awaiting their forever homes.
